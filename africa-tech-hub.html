Renvia Tech Hub Africa

Based in Akure, Nigeria, Renvia Tech Hub Africa is transforming digital access across the continent. We channel refurbished technology into schools, youth programmes, and community projects while building a physical tech hub dedicated to developing the next generation of African tech talent.

  • Coding Academy: Comprehensive coding bootcamps teaching Python, JavaScript, web development, and software engineering
  • Women in Tech Programmes: Dedicated initiatives empowering young women with technology skills and breaking barriers in the tech industry
  • Digital Literacy Bootcamps: Foundation courses building essential computer skills from basics to advanced applications
  • School Partnerships: Laptop access and technology integration for educational institutions
  • Community-Centered Development: Circular IT reuse, e-waste reduction, and sustainable technology practices

Location: Rubys Court, Oke-Ijebu, Akure, Nigeria
