Renvia Tech Hub Africa

Based in Akure, Nigeria, Renvia Tech Hub Africa channels refurbished technology into schools, youth centres, and community projects. We're building a physical tech hub to develop digital literacy, coding skills, and hardware expertise for the next generation.

  • Laptop access for schools and youth programmes
  • Digital literacy and coding pathways
  • Circular IT reuse and e-waste reduction
  • Community-centered technology development

Location: Rubys Court, Oke-Ijebu, Akure, Nigeria
