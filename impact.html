Impact in Classrooms & Communities

Our work is already creating tangible change across African communities. Here are some of the organisations and initiatives we're proud to support.

Unity Community School, Lagos

Providing 50+ refurbished laptops to support computer literacy for primary and secondary students in one of Lagos's most vibrant community schools.

Girls Code Circle, Abuja

Partnering with this grassroots initiative to equip young women with coding skills and technology access, breaking barriers in tech education.

Youth Digital Lab, Ibadan

Supporting a community-driven digital skills centre where young people learn web development, graphic design, and digital entrepreneurship.
