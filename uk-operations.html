Renvia IT Ltd (United Kingdom)

Our UK operations provide comprehensive IT asset disposal services to organisations across the country. We deliver peace of mind alongside social impact through fully compliant, secure, and environmentally responsible IT collection and processing.

  • Free Collection: Nationwide insured and licensed IT collection at no cost to your organisation
  • Full Compliance: Waste Carrier Licence, ICO registration, complete insurance coverage, and adherence to all UK regulations
  • Secure Data Handling: Professional data wiping and destruction to protect your sensitive information
  • ESG Reporting: Detailed impact reports supporting your environmental, social, and governance goals
  • Revenue Generation: Sustainable model through refurbishment, export, compliance services, and responsible recycling
